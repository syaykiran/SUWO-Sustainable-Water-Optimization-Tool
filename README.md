# SUWOT - Sustainable Water Optimization Tool

## Description
**SUWOT (Sustainable Water Optimization Tool)** is an open-source Python model designed for integrated water resource management. SUWOT effectively simulates and optimizes water allocation for various purposes, such as irrigation, hydropower, and environmental flow, under different hydrological conditions. By utilizing multi-objective genetic algorithms (MOGA), SUWOT generates **Pareto-optimal solutions**, enabling comprehensive **trade-off analyses** among competing water demands. The model's **Excel-based interface facilitates user-friendly data input** and visualization. SUWOT empowers decision-makers to explore and evaluate optimal water allocation strategies, promoting sustainable water resource management in complex river basins facing diverse and competing demands.

## Features
Integrated simulation-optimization model for water resource management.
Multi-objective genetic algorithm (MOGA) for generating Pareto-optimal solutions.
Excel-based interface for user-friendly data input and visualization.
Support for various water uses, including irrigation, hydropower, and environmental flows.
Consideration of diverse hydrological conditions and management scenarios.
Open-source code for transparency and collaboration.
